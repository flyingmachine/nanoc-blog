Usage

Setting Up Foreman

To create app groups, drag apps from finder into the white area that says "Drag apps here from finder to create new app groups."

To add apps to a group, drag apps from finder onto the group's row.

To remove apps from a group, mouse over an app and click the "x" button.

Toggling Foreman

⌘⇧. (command+shift+period) is a global hotkey to show foreman.

Activating an App Group

Activating a group launches all the apps in that group. Use your mouse to hide or close the other apps:

  • click hide other apps
  • shift+click close other apps

You can also use the keyboard:

  • ↑ and ↓ select an app group
  • ⏎ activate group and hide other apps
  • ⇧ + ⏎ activate group and close other apps

That's all there is to it! Foreman just does its thing and gets out of your way.
