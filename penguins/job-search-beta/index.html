Looking for Beta Testers

06 November 2015

Over the last couple months I've been working on a web site that helps job seekers reduce stress by following a structured, methodical process, and the site's ready for some beta testing. If you'd like to help out, please sign up for the beta test!

This isn't a unicorn-disrupting once-a-generation app, just a simple site to help people during a stressful process. For the beta test, I'm just looking for feedback on what's broken, what's confusing, and what could be useful but isn't implemented.

Thanks!

P.S. in case you're wondering, the site's all Clojure, with compojure/liberator/datomic on the backend and reagent and re-frame on the frontend.
